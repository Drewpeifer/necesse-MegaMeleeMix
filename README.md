# necesse-MegaMeleeMix

A mod for the game Necesse that adds a little something for the melee crowd

## Weapons

- The Exsanguinator (sword) - 50% change to generate 30% of total armor rating as health on hit
- The Cauterizer (greatsword) - Deals 50% of max health as fire damage per second over 10 seconds
- The Tranquilizer (hammer) - Paralyzes target for 3 seconds and delivers huge knockback
- The Boomy Whacker (spear) - Creates an explosion on EVERY swing at cursor location, scaling with weapon damage (Don't use indoors!)

## Trinkets

- The Warlord's Pauldron - 50% Resilience Gain, +25% Combat Health Regen, 100 Resilience and Max Health, -15% Attack Speed and Crit Chance, +1 Active Food Buffs
